Sports

When I was really young, I didn't have much interest in sports. However, my older brother was heavily involved in things like football and soccer, which got me interested. Ever since I was 5, I have played soccer on some sort of competitive level, and will continue to do so. Sports in general have a big impact on my life and my family too. It's been a tradition to host the Superbowl, go to a professional game (soccer or football) every year, and to be sad when the Packers lose in the NFC Championship. Without sports, I wouldn't be the person I am today.
