Academics

When I was in Pre-K and kindergarden, I struggled with academics. They were not a strongpoint of mine and were in dire need of fixing before it really started mattering. Over the course of elementary school, I gradually started to get better at everything, with the help of Kumon. By fifth grade, I was reading above average and got placed in advanced math. Now, I take almost all honors and AP classes and do well to great in all of them.
